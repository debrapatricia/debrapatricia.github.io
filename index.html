Hi!

I'm Debra Patricia, a globe-trotting college student who happens to love taking pictures and capturing memories. I grew up in Indonesia, but am now pursuing an undergraduate degree in Psychology with a minor in Art at Lookout Mountain, Georgia.

Outside of school, I love going on adventures and travelling wherever the wind takes me. It is a deep passion of mine to capture the beauty that God has endowed the world: the crashing of waves along the shores of untouched beaches in Java, the peaceful green hills and mountains in Tennessee, and the iconic skyline that adorn the magnificent city of New York.

I hope my work can convey that beauty of creation so that everyone can experience it as well. Enjoy!

DEBRA PATRICIA
